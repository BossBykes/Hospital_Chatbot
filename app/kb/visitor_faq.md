# Visiting hours
Visiting hours are 8 AM to 8 PM daily.
ICU visiting hours are 10 AM to 6 PM.

# Directions and parking
Visitor parking is available at North Wing Parking Lot A (free 2 hours).
Overflow parking is available at South Garage on Elm St (paid parking).

# Billing and insurance
You can pay your bill online at https://hospital.example.com/pay or at the cashier desk (Ground floor).
We accept Aetna, BCBS, and UnitedHealthcare.

# Cafeteria
Cafeteria hours:
- Weekdays: 6 AM to 8 PM
- Weekends: 7 AM to 7 PM

# Emergency
In an emergency, call your local emergency number.
If you are on-site, contact the hospital emergency desk immediately.
